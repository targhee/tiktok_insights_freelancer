The Push Settings is an app setting TikTokInsights can't see, so we can only offer suggestions

You will need to check your app, but we'll help guide you.


Open TikTok and make sure Push Settings are set.

TikTok measures how engaged you are with your viewers - especially in the hours after you post

a new video. The faster you respond, the more engaged viewers feel and the higher TikTok will

rank you. The Push settings will notify you when you get a like, comment or share so you can

respond.


Remember, the first few hours after posting are critical for engaging with your audience.
